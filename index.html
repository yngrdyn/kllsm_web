Kolliseum is not a dating app.

It’s a hangout app!



Sometimes we are alone and have some time to kill. All we want to do is hang out for a limited period of time, maybe 30 minutes or an hour.

Kolliseum allows you to find a social activity in your vicinity and connect with someone to meet briefly and instantly. You can have a coffee, go to the movies or for a short run or bike ride.

All you have to do is look at the map, say what you want to do and connect with someone.

You don’t need to wait for organized activities or meetups. Kolliseum is for something brief and immediate. It’s not a dating app! It’s a hangout app!

How it works



Sign up and find out fun things to do in your vicinity

Create or select the activity you want to do.

Connect with someone and set up a meeting place.
Meet, greet, hangout, and say goodbye.

Rate the social experience and Repeat!
