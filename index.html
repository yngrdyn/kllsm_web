• About Us

It's for people who have some free time

and want to do something with new

people in a friendly environment.



Every now and then we find ourselves alone (when commuting, traveling for work, lunch time). All we want to do is hang out for a limited period of time, 30 minutes or maybe an hour.

Kolliseum allows you to find a social activity in your vicinity and connect with someone to meet briefly and immediately (for example, taking pictures, having a coffee, going to the movies, for a bike ride or a run, or any other unplanned meetup).

In Kolliseum you say what you want to do and how much time you have available, or simply find out what somebody else is doing to join them and hang out.

How it works



Do you have some available time for a social activity?
Sign up and find out who else is around.

Create an activity to see who wants to join you, or search to see what others are doing for you to join them.

Set up a meeting with someone whose social activity is interesting to you.
The chat disappears in 10 minutes.
